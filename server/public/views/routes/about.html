About Us

In 2004, Muffy Baker started Monumental Muffins to provide fresh, healthy muffins, made of all-natural ingredients to her catering clientele.

The muffins were a hit, and Muffy began to add new flavors and perfect her recipes to include only the finest ingredients.

Since the muffin venture continued to grow, Muffy moved the company to our current location in 2009, ended her other catering business, and focused solely on creating truly MONUMENTAL muffins!

Come by, and try some fresh-baked, delicious muffins!
